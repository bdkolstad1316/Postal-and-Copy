About us

Postal & Copy + (Postal and Copy Plus) is a locally owned pack-and-ship, print, and business-services center on 3rd Street in Clarkston, Washington, serving the Lewiston–Clarkston Valley. As a FedEx Authorized ShipCenter and USPS-contracted location, we make shipping simple — and we don’t stop there. We build custom-sized boxes on-site, print everything from business cards and blueprints to 24″ banners, offer free notary service, and even keep a gift shop of unique, reasonably priced finds. Friendly staff, quick service, and fair prices — we treat every order like it’s our own.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Postal & Copy + ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at postalandcopyplus.com. This policy explains what information this website collects and how it is used. It applies to this website only — not to in-store services or transactions.

1. Information we collect

We do not ask for or store personal information through this website. There is no account system and no contact form. We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment details on this site.

Analytics. We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics service, to understand overall site traffic — such as which pages are viewed, general location (country/region level), referring sites, and device type. Plausible does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track you across other websites or over time. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

This website does not set its own cookies, and our analytics are cookieless. Note that the embedded Google Map on our Hours & Location section is provided by Google and may set its own cookies or collect data under Google's Privacy Policy.

When you follow these links or interact with embedded content, the third party's own privacy policy applies. We do not control and are not responsible for their practices.

4. How we use information

Aggregate, anonymous analytics help us improve the website and understand what visitors find useful. We do not sell, rent, or share visitor data.

5. Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

6. Your choices

Because we do not collect personal data on this site, there is nothing to access or delete. You can block analytics at any time using a standard browser content blocker.

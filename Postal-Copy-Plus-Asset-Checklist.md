# Postal & Copy + — "Next Level" Asset & Info Checklist

**Prepared for:** Brian (KD70) · **Re:** postalandcopyplus.com · **Date:** June 28, 2026
**Doubles as the agenda for the meeting with Vince (owner), Tue June 30.**

The website is live and solid, but it's running on **zero real photos** and several
assumed details. This checklist is everything that would take it from "good small
business site" to "best-in-town." Items are grouped by priority. The biggest wins are
in Priority 1.

---

## Priority 1 — Photography (the single biggest gap)

The site currently has **no real photos**. For a local retail + service business, real
imagery is the #1 driver of trust and walk-in traffic. Phone-camera shots are fine — shoot
in natural light, landscape orientation, high-res (2000px+ wide). We'll crop/optimize.

- [ ] **Storefront exterior** with the sign visible — helps people recognize and find you
- [ ] **Interior** — counter, retail floor, the overall feel of the shop
- [ ] **Gift displays** — 6–10 well-lit shots (feeds the new Featured Gifts gallery)
- [ ] **Owner / staff** — a friendly photo of Vince and the team (humanizes a local biz)
- [ ] **Action shots** — packing a box, printing a banner, notarizing, the box builder in use
- [ ] **Equipment** — wide-format printer, copiers (shows real capability)
- [ ] Optional: mailbox area, passport-photo setup

## Priority 1 — Logo & Brand Files

- [ ] **Official logo** — vector (SVG / AI / EPS / PDF) **and** a high-res transparent PNG
      *(the site currently uses a text "P+" placeholder mark)*
- [ ] **Exact brand colors** (hex codes) if they exist — we're currently approximating navy + red
- [ ] **Brand font(s)**, if any
- [ ] Existing **print materials** (business card, flyer, signage photo) to match the look
- [ ] Official **tagline / positioning** line, if there is one

## Priority 2 — Business Details to Confirm (accuracy)

- [ ] Exact **carrier authorizations** and wording (FedEx Authorized ShipCenter; for USPS — is
      it a Contract Postal Unit / Approved Postal Provider?)
- [ ] **Full current service list** + anything no longer offered (passport photos? fingerprinting?)
- [ ] **Amazon / online returns** — do you accept them? *(A huge traffic driver for ship stores —
      currently not mentioned anywhere on the site.)*

- [ ] **Mailbox rental** — pricing/plans, package acceptance, access hours
- [ ] **Printing** — do you want prices, a rate sheet, or a "request a quote" flow?
- [ ] **Year established / history** — how long in business, family-owned? (for a real About story)
- [ ] **Service area** wording (e.g., Lewiston–Clarkston Valley)
- [ ] **Payment methods** accepted
- [ ] **Holiday / seasonal hours**
- [ ] Languages spoken (if relevant)

## Priority 3 — Off-Site & Blind Spots (not on the site, but critical)

These are the things small businesses most often miss — and several matter more than the
website itself.

- [ ] **Claim the Google Business Profile** (currently **unclaimed**) — add website, photos,
      hours, services, posts. For a local store this drives *more* traffic than the website.
- [ ] **Email on the domain** (info@postalandcopyplus.com) — not set up yet; needed for a
      contact form and a professional address. (Cloudflare Email Routing is the quick path.)
- [ ] **Contact form** — the site is currently call/visit only; there's no way to message
      online. Decide yes/no (requires email above).
- [ ] **Analytics** — no traffic visibility today. Add Google Analytics or privacy-friendly
      Plausible, plus Google Search Console.
- [ ] **Directory consistency (NAP)** — make Name/Address/Phone identical across Yelp, Bing
      Places, Apple Business Connect, the FedEx locator, and PackageHub.
- [ ] **Social media** — Facebook / Instagram handles to link (many local ship + gift stores
      live on Facebook). Provide handles, or decide to create them.
- [ ] **Privacy policy** — needed once analytics, email, or a form is added.
- [ ] **Holiday shipping deadlines** — a seasonal page/post; big value for a ship store.
- [ ] **FAQ page** — Amazon returns? passport photos? notary fee? "can you receive my
      packages?" do you sell boxes? (Cuts phone calls and helps local SEO.)
